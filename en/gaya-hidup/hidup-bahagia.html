Living happily is the goal of all people. Unfortunately, many people make mistakes in chasing happiness by breaking norms and laws. Fortunately, there is a simple way, including through the use of healthy, legal, and productive computers.

However, we certainly want to be grateful that there is a **Linus Torvalds** and the FOSS community who brought Linux. Safe from viruses, freely used, freely learnable, and freely shareable. Research also shows that sharing can increase someone's happiness.
