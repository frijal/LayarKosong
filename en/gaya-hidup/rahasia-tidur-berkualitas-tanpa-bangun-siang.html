How to Get Quality Sleep? 🚀

Here is what you're eagerly waiting for. How to get short but deep sleep? Here's the clinical step:

  • **Block Blue Light:** Turn off your phone at least 1 hour before bedtime. The blue light from your phone makes your brain think it's still daytime, so melatonin doesn't get released.
  • **Take a Warm Bath:** This is a biological trick. After taking a warm bath, the body's core temperature drops drastically when you leave the bathroom. This temperature drop is the natural signal to your brain that it's time for a deep sleep.
  • **Magnesium & Nutrition:** Eat foods rich in magnesium like nuts or bananas during the afternoon to help muscles and nerves relax.
  • **Mind Dump:** If your mind is still active, write down all your worries on paper before sleep. Empty your 'RAM' of the brain so you can enter the Deep Sleep phase faster.
