Kenapa (Why) Ini Berbahaya bagi Jantung dan Metabolisme? 🫀

**2. Metabolic Disorders and Diabetes:** This is the most common occurrence. Sleeping after dawn disrupts insulin sensitivity. The body is not efficiently processing blood sugar in the morning when it should be active. The result? Accumulation of fat in the abdomen (visceral obesity) and the risk of Type 2 diabetes significantly increases. So, it's not surprising if your stomach gets bigger even when you've cut back on eating, possibly due to your habit of sleeping in the morning!

Dampaknya bagi Kesehatan Mental dan Fokus (Where & How) 🧠

Impact on Mental Health and Focus (Where & How) 🧠

Where is this impact felt the most? In your brain. Sleeping in the morning deprives you of the **Oxygen-Rich Phase** that is abundant in the morning hours (as discussed in the Wake-up early benefits article). An oxygen-deprived brain in the morning is more prone to depression and anxiety.

How does it work? Sleeping in the morning disrupts the production of *Serotonin*. Serotonin is the chemical neurotransmitter in the brain that makes you feel happy and calm. If its production is disrupted due to irregular sleep schedules, it's not surprising that you feel irritable or empty-minded all day, with no clear reason. ⛈️
