January 1, 2026 ·

Public Health, BPJS Kesehatan

Starting **January 1, 2026**, BPJS Kesehatan will enforce a new rule that all JKN-KIS participants must comply with. From now on, each JKN-KIS participant must conduct a **Health History Screening (SRK)** at least once a year before accessing services at Level 1 health facilities (FKTP).

Rest assured, this is not an administrative trap. Quite the opposite — this screening is designed as a preventive measure to detect potential non-communicable diseases before they become serious. And good news: it's **free 💸**.

🎯 Objectives of the Health History Screening

This screening aims to detect risks for **14 types of non-communicable diseases** including diabetes, hypertension, coronary heart disease, stroke, and certain types of cancer. In essence, BPJS is shifting its focus from "treating" to "preventing."

  1. 📱 Online Method for the Health History Screening for BPJS Participants
  2. 1️⃣ Through the Mobile JKN App (Most Recommended)
  3. Open the **Mobile JKN** app
  4. Pilih participants (can include family members)
  5. Fill out all the questions honestly (around 16-47 questions)
  6. Results appear right away

2️⃣ Through the Official BPJS Kesehatan Website

  1. Visit **https://screening.bpjs-kesehatan.go.id**
  2. Enter your BPJS card number, date of birth, and captcha
  3. Complete the questionnaire
  4. Send and see the screening results

📊 Reading the Screening Results

  • **Low Risk 🟢** – Maintain a healthy lifestyle
  • **Moderate Risk 🟡** – Recommended for further examinations at FKTP
  • **High Risk 🔴** – Immediate consultation and medical examination

💡 Why This Screening Is Required?

According to the latest BPJS Kesehatan regulations, screening is now a **mandatory administrative requirement** before participants can receive doctor visits at FKTP. Without annual screenings, service access can be delayed.

Additionally, this screening data serves as a reference for healthcare professionals for long-term health monitoring. It's not just formalities, but actually used.

☎️ If You Encounter Any Issues

If you encounter any technical issues, participants can contact:

  • **Care Center 165**
  • **JKN Chat Assistant (CHIKA)**

In today's digital and preventive era, this BPJS health screening is essentially a small step with a large impact. It takes only a few minutes, without any cost, but can determine the long-term quality of life.
