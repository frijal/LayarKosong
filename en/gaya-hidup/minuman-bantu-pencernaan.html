💧 1. Warm Water

This is the simplest and most effective. Drinking a glass of **warm water in the morning** when your stomach is empty can stimulate intestinal movement and boost metabolism.

🥛 2. Probiotic Yogurt

Yogurt contains **good bacteria (probiotics)** that help maintain a balanced gut flora. Regular consumption of yogurt without added sugar can prevent constipation and improve the digestive system.

🍋 3. Warm Lemon Water

A mixture of warm water with a bit of lemon juice can aid in light detoxification and stimulate digestive enzymes. This drink also helps maintain hydration throughout the day.

🥬 4. Green Juice

Juices from green vegetables like spinach, celery, or cucumber are rich in water-soluble fiber and natural enzymes. Besides being refreshing, this juice aids in expediting the food digestion process in the intestines.

🍎 5. High-Fiber Fruit Juice

Jus dari sayuran hijau seperti bayam, seledri, atau timun kaya akan serat larut dan enzim alami. Selain menyegarkan, jus ini membantu mempercepat proses pencernaan makanan di usus.
