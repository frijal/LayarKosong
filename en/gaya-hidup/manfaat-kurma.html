Why is Mango Good for Your Health?

Mango is a fruit commonly consumed during iftar and as a healthy snack. Here are the main benefits commonly known:

1. Quick Energy Source

Mango contains natural sugars like glucose, fructose, and sucrose, providing quick energy, suitable for restoring energy when the body needs it.

2. Rich in Fiber

Fiber in mango aids in digestive processes, preventing constipation, and supporting healthy gut microbiota.

3. Contains Minerals and Vitamins

Mango contains potassium, magnesium, copper, vitamin B6, and antioxidants - beneficial for nerve function, blood pressure, and immune system.

4. Antioxidants

Polyphenols and other antioxidants in mango help combat free radicals that can damage cells. This can contribute to a reduced risk of chronic diseases when consumed as part of a healthy diet.

5. Heart Health

The fiber and potassium content in mango help maintain blood pressure and cholesterol levels, important factors for cardiovascular health.

Consumption Tips

  • Consume in moderate amounts as mango is high in calories and natural sugars.
