1. You've Reached the Age Requirement (Essential!)

Driving isn't a game, but a great responsibility. The first and foremost requirement is that you are of **legal driving age**. This isn't just about the numbers, but about mental maturity and emotional control on the road.

This is clearly stated in **Article 81, Paragraph 1-4 of Law No. 22 of 2009 on Traffic and Transportation**. So, if you haven't reached the age requirement, better enjoy being a passenger for now, okay? 👶
