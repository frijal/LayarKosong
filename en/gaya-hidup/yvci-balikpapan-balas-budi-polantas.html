Solidaritas Jalanan: YVCI Balikpapan 'Balas Budi' Petugas Polantas

Published: November 30, 2025 • By: Personal Data Protection Unit

Who says motor clubs are only for touring and hangouts? This time, the **Yamaha Vixion Club Indonesia (YVCI) Balikpapan Chapter** demonstrated that their social spirit is just as strong as the roar of the engine! This 'Balas Budi' action is more than just giving out snacks; it's also a genuine expression of gratitude from the community of road users for our traffic heroes. Impressive! Let's take a closer look at the full story. 👇

YVCI Balikpapan Chapter: 'Balas Budi' Service to Traffic Police Officers

As a way of showing appreciation for the dedication of traffic police officers who continuously serve and secure road traffic every day, **Yamaha Vixion Club Indonesia (YVCI) Balikpapan Chapter** organized an event called 'Balas Budi', distributing simple snacks to officers at several police posts in Balikpapan.

Here is the translated text with preserved HTML tags and internal links rewritten to `/en/`:

Kunjungan ke Pos Polantas Balikpapan Baru. Siap siaga mengatur lalu lintas kota minyak. 🚦

Suasana hangat saat YVCI Balikpapan berbagi di Pos Polantas Simpang Rapak.

In this simple package, there is a sincere thank you. To be a Polatar is not an easy job. Besides dealing with pollution and extreme weather conditions, they also have to be patient in attending to complaints and resolving traffic jams. The "Balas Budi" act serves as a reminder that respecting the service of public servants on the roads is a shared responsibility, as fellow road users. May this activity inspire other communities to join in good deeds! ✨
**"Sorry to only be able to provide a little help. The challenge is immense to repay all the service and dedication of our police officers, who daily toil in the heat to ensure our safety and smooth flow on the roads."**Thank You, Police Officers!

Keep Safety Riding & Keep Safety on Duty!
Bakti Sosial towards Police Officers*
