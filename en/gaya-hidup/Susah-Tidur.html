Apa yang dimaksud dengan "susah tidur"?

Secara sederhana, susah tidur (insomnia ringkas) adalah kesulitan untuk jatuh tertidur, sering terbangun di malam hari, atau terbangun terlalu pagi dan tidak bisa kembali tidur. Bila terjadi lebih sering dan mengganggu fungsi harian, sebaiknya dicari penyebabnya dan konsultasi ke profesional kesehatan.

Langkah praktis yang bisa dicoba malam ini:

1. Atur rutinitas (konsistensi) ⏰

Tidur dan bangun di jam yang sama setiap hari membantu mengatur jam biologis (sirkadian). Usahakan konsisten termasuk akhir pekan.

2. Ciptakan lingkungan tidur yang mendukung 🌑

  • Ruang gelap, tenang, dan sejuk, suhu kamar yang lebih sejuk membantu tidur.

3. Kurangi kafein & alkohol ☕

Gun up and use comfortable bedding; do not use the bedroom for work.

3. Reduce caffeine & alcohol ☕

Avoid coffee, caffeinated tea, energy drinks for several hours before bedtime; alcohol can disrupt sleep cycles even if it induces initial drowsiness.

4. Limit screens & blue light 📱

Turn off or use "Night Mode" at least 1 hour before bedtime. Blue light from screens can suppress melatonin and delay the feeling of drowsiness.

5. Try short relaxation techniques 🧘

Try square breathing (4-4-4-4), progressive muscle relaxation, or mindfulness (imagine a warm and calm room).

6. If you wake up at night 🌃

  • Do not keep staring at the clock. If you don't fall asleep within ~20 minutes, get up, do a calming activity (reading a physical book, deep breathing), and return to bed when you feel drowsy.
  • ☀️ Daily steps supporting sleep
  • Get natural sunlight exposure in the morning (helps regulate your circadian rhythm).

Regular physical activity (avoid intense exercise right before bedtime).

Watch your evening meals, do not eat too close to bedtime.

Sleep hygiene = sleep habits and environment supporting sleep. For example, a consistent sleep schedule, a dark and cool environment, and reducing stimuli before sleep.

Is it safe to take sleeping pills?

Sleeping pills are sometimes prescribed for short-term use, but they have a risk of dependency and side effects. Discuss with a doctor before routinely using sleeping pills.

Does meditation help?

Many people feel that meditation and breathing exercises help reduce anxiety and facilitate sleep.
