Kenapa Seseorang Tidak Buang Air Besar Setiap Hari?

There are several reasons why someone might not have to urinate every day. Don't panic, this is quite common and can be caused by various factors.

🩺 Possible Causes

  1. **Insufficient fiber:** A diet low in fiber can lead to constipation or difficulty in passing stool.
  2. **Not drinking enough:** Dehydration causes hard, compacted stool.
  3. **Lack of physical activity:** A sedentary lifestyle slows down the digestive system.
  4. **Stress:** Stress can disrupt the digestive rhythm and cause constipation.
  5. **Medication side effects:** Certain medications can also cause constipation.
  6. **Medical conditions:** For example, hypothyroidism, diabetes, or gastrointestinal disorders.

If you are concerned about the frequency of urination, it's best to consult a doctor to determine the exact cause and receive appropriate advice.

💩 About Stool

Stool is what remains of food that the body cannot digest. Before exiting, stool is stored in the large intestine (colon) and rectum. The colon absorbs water and electrolytes from the food residue, making the stool more compact. The rectum serves as a temporary storage area before the stool is eventually expelled through the anus.

  • 📍 Position of the Colon in the Body
  • The colon (large intestine) has a shape like a large frame surrounding the small intestine and it is about **1.5 meters** long. Its order roughly goes like this:
  • Starting from the bottom right of the stomach (umbilical area), it ascends upwards → **ascending colon**
  • Then it lies horizontally below the stomach → **transverse colon**

Then it descends on the left side → **descending colon**

Finally, it leads to the **rectum** and **anus**
