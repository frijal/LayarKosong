Jambore Vixion with Honda Sponsor 😱 Heated, Big Talk!

May 7, 2016

Hey bro, fellow bikers, remember this legendary moment in Balikpapan? 🔥 The Jambore Yamaha Vixion across Borneo (YVCI) held in 2016 suddenly became a topic of discussion among Indonesian motorcycle communities. Imagine: a Vixion community event closely associated with Yamaha, yet its main sponsor was... Honda! 😱

This phenomenon surprised many, as motorcycle communities are usually very loyal to their brands. Yamaha events are typically sponsored by Yamaha, Honda by Honda. But this time, things were different — Honda attributes dominated the event area, from large Honda banners to the distinctive red Wing activation. It's no wonder that discussions exploded on social media, with many asking, "Where's Yamaha? How did this happen?" 🤔😂
