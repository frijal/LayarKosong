🩺 1. Listen to Your Body

Everyone has a different digestive rhythm. Having bowel movements every day doesn't necessarily mean it's a problem, but if you start feeling discomfort, hardness, or bloating, it's a sign your gut needs more attention.

🥕 2. Prioritize Fiber and Fluid

Fiber from fruits, vegetables, and whole grains helps move waste through the intestines, while fluids keep it from becoming too hard. A combination of both is key to regular bowel movements without drama.

🥛 3. Understand Dairy and Non-Dairy Effects

Dairy products can affect digestion — some work well, while others may cause constipation. Try switching from cow's milk to options like **almond milk**, **oat milk**, or **soy milk**, and notice the difference. Your body will let you know if it likes or dislikes them.
